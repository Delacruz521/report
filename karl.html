Generosity

You're not born generous. But if you consistently share your time, resources, or kindness with others, you gradually develop generosity as a character trait.

Temperance

You learn temperance by practicing self-control repeatedly. It's not about never having treats, but about making conscious choices to avoid excess.

Courage

Courage isn't the absence of fear, but acting despite fear. You build courage by facing your fears and challenging yourself.
